Travel Tips and Tricks

  1. Stay Hydrated

    Whether you're in the streets of Ravnica or the jungles of Ixalan, staying hydrated is very important.

  2. Keep Extra Rations With You

    It is always useful to keep extra rations with you. You never know when you won't be able to find food.

  3. Learn to Forage and Hunt

    You cannot keep an indefinite amount of food and water with you, so make sure you know how to forage and hunt.

  4. Stay Armed

    You never know when you could be attacked by a vampire. Or a zombie. Or a demon.

  5. Stay Alert

    Trust your gut. If something seems dangerous, that's probably because it is.

  6. Avoid Vampires

    Never ask a vampire of Ixalan about his religion unless you want to become immortal against your will. (Believe me, being immortal isn't as cool as it sounds.)

  7. Never Buy Anything From a Demon

    I honestly wouldn't mind buying from them, but I wish I could just pay with money, rather than my soul. Y'know, like EVERY OTHER BUSINESS.

  8. Never Borrow Money From the Orzhov Syndicate

    They'll make sure you can't pay it back, so that when you die, they own your soul.
